Lakers Today

Current News on The Los Angeles Lakers!

1. The Lakers are currently 30-26, sitting at 9th in the Western Conference in the NBA. This puts them in contention for the NBA Play-In Tournament prior to the NBA Playoffs, but with 26 games left in the regular season, a lot can still change.

2. LeBron James and Anthony Davis just recently participated in the 2024 NBA All-Star Game in Indianapolis, where the former was selected as his team's captain as well as starting small forward. They have until Feb. 22 before they play their next game.

3. Lakers' players Jarred Vanderbilt, Gabe Vincent, Max Christie, and Cam Reddish are all currently on the injury report, but there is optimism that all four players will be healthy for the postseason.
